Getting Started
How does LiftPitch work? ▾
LiftPitch lets you record a short, live-verified video pitch and share it via a link on your resume. Here's the flow:
  1. 1Paste your resume and a job description to get a match score and personalized script.
  2. 2Use the Strengthen Your Resume tool to address any gaps identified.
  3. 3Record your live video pitch directly in your browser — no uploads allowed.
  4. 4Copy your verified link and add it to your resume.

Video Recording Issues
My camera shows a black screen — what do I do? ▾
A black camera screen is almost always caused by one of these:
  1. 1Update your browser. An outdated version of Chrome is the most common cause. Go to Chrome menu → Help → About Google Chrome to check for updates.
  2. 2Check camera permissions. Your browser needs permission to access your camera. Look for a camera icon in the address bar and click "Allow."
  3. 3Close other apps using your camera. Zoom, FaceTime, or other video apps may have your camera locked. Close them and try again.
  4. 4Try a different browser. Chrome and Edge work best. Safari on Mac also works well.
  5. 5Restart your browser. Close it completely and reopen the page.
Note: LiftPitch requires a physical camera on your device. Virtual cameras or screen-sharing software may not work.
How do I allow camera and microphone access? ▾
🌐 Chrome
Click the camera icon in the address bar → select "Always allow" → refresh the page.
🧭 Safari
Safari menu → Settings for This Website → Camera & Microphone → set to "Allow."
🦊 Firefox
Click the camera icon in the address bar → select "Allow" for both camera and microphone.
📱 Mobile
Go to your phone's Settings → Privacy → Camera → enable access for your browser app.
Can I upload a pre-recorded video? ▾
No — and this is intentional. LiftPitch only allows live, in-browser recordings. This is our core trust and verification feature: every video gets a "Live Verified" badge that tells recruiters the pitch was recorded in real time by a real person. Pre-recorded or AI-generated videos are not accepted.

Script Generator Issues
The page is spinning and won't load — how do I fix it? ▾
This is usually caused by your browser serving a cached (saved) old version of the site. Here's how to fix it:
🌐 Chrome
Press Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) for a hard reload. Or open an Incognito window (Cmd+Shift+N) and try again.
🧭 Safari
Hold Shift and click the Reload button. Or go to Develop menu → Empty Caches, then reload.
📱 Mobile
Close the browser app completely, reopen it, and navigate back to lift-pitch.co.
Still spinning after 10 seconds? Try opening LiftPitch in a private or incognito window. If it loads there, clearing your browser cache will fix it in your regular window. Contact us at support@lift-pitch.co if the issue continues.
My resume looks garbled after pasting — what happened? ▾
Copying and pasting from a PDF can distort the formatting — words may merge together or lines may be out of order. For best results:
  1. 1Use the Upload Word Doc button to upload your resume as a .docx file.
  2. 2Or copy from a plain text version of your resume if you have one.
  3. 3If you only have a PDF, try pasting it and then manually cleaning up any obvious formatting errors before generating.
Do I have to use the script? ▾
Not at all! The script is a starting point, not a requirement. Many users find it most useful to review the match score and gap analysis to understand what to highlight in their pitch, and then speak naturally in their own words. The script is there if you need help getting started — but recruiters respond best to authentic, conversational delivery. Use it however feels right for you.

Sharing Your Pitch
How do I add my pitch link to my resume? ▾
After recording, copy your verified link from the Record page. Add it to your resume near your name and contact information — for example:

Video Pitch: lift-pitch.co/v/your-link-here
You can also add it as a hyperlink on your name or a "View My Pitch" line. Recruiters who click the link will see your video with a Live Verified badge.
What do recruiters see when they click my link? ▾
Recruiters see your video with a Live Verified badge confirming it was recorded live in real time. Free tier videos include a LiftPitch watermark. Pro users get a clean, watermark-free experience. Recruiters do not need a LiftPitch account to watch your video.
How long does my video stay active? ▾
Videos are stored for 12 months from the recording date. After that the link will expire. You can request early deletion at any time by emailing support@lift-pitch.co. We recommend re-recording your pitch periodically to keep it current.
